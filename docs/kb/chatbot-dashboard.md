A **complete, structured breakdown of chatbot metrics**

---

# 1. Usage & Adoption Metrics

**Purpose:** Measure how much the chatbot is being used and by whom.

### Core Metrics

* **Conversations**

  * Total number of chat sessions started
  * Indicates overall demand and workload
* **Active Users**

  * Unique users who interacted with the bot
  * Shows reach and adoption
* **New vs Returning Users**

  * New users = first-time interactions
  * Returning users = repeat engagement
* **Conversations per User**

  * Average sessions per user
  * Helps detect usability and reliance
* **Daily / Weekly / Monthly Active Users (DAU/WAU/MAU)**

  * Tracks consistency of usage over time
* **Peak Usage Times**

  * Hours or days with highest traffic
  * Important for scaling and staffing

---

# 2. Engagement Metrics

**Purpose:** Understand how users interact with the chatbot.

### Conversation Quality

* **Messages per Conversation**

  * Average turns in a session
  * Too high = confusion, too low = shallow help
* **Conversation Duration**

  * Time spent per session
* **Drop-off Rate**

  * Users leaving mid-conversation
* **Completion Rate**

  * % of conversations reaching a clear end
* **Bounce Rate**

  * Users who leave after the first message

---

# 3. Performance & Speed Metrics

**Purpose:** Measure technical efficiency and responsiveness.

### System Performance

* **Response Time**

  * Time taken to reply (e.g., 0.8s)
  * Strongly affects user satisfaction
* **First Response Time**

  * Time before the bot’s first reply
* **Latency Distribution**

  * p50 / p95 / p99 response times
* **Uptime / Availability**

  * % of time the bot is operational
* **Error Rate**

  * Failed messages, API errors, timeouts

---

# 4. Resolution & Effectiveness Metrics

**Purpose:** Measure how well the chatbot solves problems.

### Resolution Outcomes

* **Resolution Rate**

  * % of conversations successfully solved
* **Automated Resolution Rate**

  * Issues resolved without human help (e.g., 73%)
* **Escalation Rate**

  * % handed off to human agents (e.g., 14%)
* **Unresolved Rate**

  * Conversations ending without resolution (e.g., 13%)
* **First Contact Resolution (FCR)**

  * Issues solved in a single conversation
* **Retry Rate**

  * Users asking the same question again later

---

# 5. Customer Satisfaction & Feedback Metrics

**Purpose:** Measure user perception and experience.

### User Feedback

* **CSAT (Customer Satisfaction Score)**

  * Star or numeric rating (e.g., 4.8/5)
* **Feedback Response Rate**

  * % of users who leave feedback
* **Positive vs Negative Feedback Ratio**
* **Thumbs Up / Thumbs Down Rate**
* **Comment Sentiment**

  * NLP-based sentiment analysis
* **Post-Resolution Satisfaction**

  * Satisfaction after an issue is marked resolved

---

# 6. Intent & Knowledge Metrics

**Purpose:** Understand what users are asking and how well the bot understands.

### Intent Analytics

* **Most Asked Questions**

  * Top intents (e.g., password reset, billing)
* **Intent Frequency**

  * How often each intent appears
* **Intent Trends**

  * Growth or decline over time
* **New / Unknown Intents**

  * Questions the bot doesn’t recognize
* **Intent Match Confidence**

  * How confident the model is in intent detection
* **Fallback Rate**

  * When the bot says “I didn’t understand that”

---

# 7. Knowledge Base & Content Metrics

**Purpose:** Measure how effective your content is.

### Knowledge Effectiveness

* **Knowledge Article Usage**

  * Which articles are used most
* **Answer Success Rate**

  * % of times an article resolves the issue
* **Outdated Content Rate**

  * Content triggering poor feedback
* **Search-to-Answer Ratio**

  * How often search leads to resolution
* **Content Gap Analysis**

  * High-demand questions with no good answers

---

# 8. AI & Model Quality Metrics

**Purpose:** Evaluate AI understanding and learning quality.

### AI Accuracy

* **Intent Accuracy**

  * Correct intent classification rate
* **Entity Extraction Accuracy**

  * Correct identification of names, IDs, dates
* **Confidence Score Distribution**
* **Hallucination Rate**

  * Incorrect or fabricated responses
* **Model Drift**

  * Performance change over time
* **Training Coverage**

  * % of intents trained vs total observed

---

# 9. Human Handoff & Agent Metrics

**Purpose:** Measure collaboration between bot and human agents.

### Escalation Performance

* **Time to Agent Handoff**
* **Agent Resolution Time**
* **Bot-to-Agent Context Accuracy**

  * Whether agents receive correct conversation context
* **Agent Override Rate**

  * How often agents must correct the bot
* **Agent Satisfaction Score**

  * Internal feedback from support staff

---

# 10. Business & ROI Metrics

**Purpose:** Measure business impact and value.

### Cost & Value

* **Cost per Conversation**
* **Cost Savings**

  * Compared to human-only support
* **Deflection Rate**

  * % of tickets avoided due to automation
* **Revenue Influence**

  * Upsells, renewals, retention via chatbot
* **Churn Reduction**

  * Customers retained due to faster support
* **Support Ticket Reduction**

  * Volume decrease after chatbot deployment

---

# 11. Compliance, Safety & Trust Metrics

**Purpose:** Ensure reliability, safety, and compliance.

### Governance

* **PII Detection Rate**

  * Identification of sensitive data
* **Policy Violation Rate**
* **Blocked / Restricted Responses**
* **Audit Logs**

  * Conversation traceability
* **User Consent Rate**
* **Data Retention Compliance**

---

# 12. Trend & Comparative Metrics

**Purpose:** Measure progress and improvements over time.

### Trends

* **Period-over-Period Change**

  * (e.g., +22.9% conversations)
* **Cohort Analysis**

  * Behavior of user groups over time
* **Before vs After Changes**

  * Impact of new intents, KB updates
* **Seasonality Patterns**

---

## How Your Current Dashboard Fits In

Dashboard should covers:

* Usage (Conversations, Active Users)
* Performance (Response Time)
* Satisfaction (CSAT)
* Resolution (Automated, Escalated, Unresolved)
* Intent analysis (Most Asked Questions)
* AI accuracy & fallback metrics
* Business ROI metrics
* Knowledge base effectiveness
* Agent collaboration quality
* Compliance & safety metrics
